Nearby Places of the Temple

Place 1: Shree Hanuman Mandir

Location: New Delhi

Distance: 1.2 km

The Shree Hanuman Mandir is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located in the heart of New Delhi. The temple is known for its serene atmosphere, attracting devotees from all over the city. Visitors often come to seek blessings for strength and perseverance. The temple is beautifully adorned with vibrant paintings and sculptures depicting stories from Hindu mythology. The peaceful surroundings make it a perfect place for contemplation and devotion.

Timing: 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Place 2: Kalkaji Mandir

Location: New Delhi

Distance: 3.5 km

Kalkaji Mandir is one of the most important temples in Delhi, dedicated to Goddess Kali. Situated near the Kalkaji metro station, the temple is a prime destination for devotees and tourists alike. The grand architecture and the tranquil ambiance make it a popular spot for religious and spiritual gatherings. The temple is believed to be over a thousand years old, with a rich history of worship and devotion. The temple also serves as a place of spiritual learning and growth, with regular prayers and rituals.

Timing: 5:30 AM - 10:00 PM

View Map

Place 3: Akshardham Temple

Location: New Delhi

Distance: 4.0 km

The Akshardham Temple in New Delhi is a majestic temple dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan. It showcases the grandeur of traditional Indian culture, architecture, and spirituality. The temple complex includes exhibitions on the history of India, a boat ride through Indian civilization, and a giant statue of Swaminarayan that attracts thousands of visitors every day. Akshardham is a marvel of art and devotion, offering an immersive experience that reflects the divine presence of Lord Swaminarayan.

Timing: 9:30 AM - 8:00 PM
